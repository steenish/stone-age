Consent

In this task, you will be presented with computer-generated imagery of stone surfaces. We are interested in seeing how you rate the realism and appeal of these images. The task will take approximately 3 minutes.


By clicking "Continue" below, you agree to take part in this study. Data from this study will be be kept confidential and only shown in aggregate form. This study is conducted in accordance with the ethical guidelines of KTH Royal Institute of Technology.


Instructions

In this task, you will be shown a computer-generated image of a stone surface with its original appearance. At the same time, you will see two images side by side, both showing the stone surface's appearance after aging for a given period of time.


Your task is to answer which of the two aged appearances is more realistic to you, and which one is more appealing to you. You answer by clicking on the button associated with the image. In this context, you are free to decide what realism means to you and answer accordingly.


There will be a total of 18 image pairs, each needing to be rated in terms of realism and appeal.
